About Us

Who We Are

SriStar is your one-stop solution for quick cash and profitable gold investment opportunities. We offer the best value for your precious metals because we understand how important they are to you.

Founded by Mr Srikanth V. Nalige and Mr Manjappa Bidaragad in 2019, we are an ISO Certified company aiming to become India’s most trusted name in gold buying and selling.

We have 10+ offices across Karnataka and Tamil Nadu. Can’t visit us? Don’t worry — we offer secure doorstep services too!

What We Do

Whether you're planning to buy property, start your dream venture, own your favorite car, or travel abroad but facing a cash shortage, SriStar Gold Company is here to help. We buy new, old, and scrap gold at current market rates and offer gold loan interest payments, pledge release from banks and financiers, and quick, transparent service for your satisfaction.

We also provide gold jewelry on rent and sell a wide variety of traditional, vintage, and modern designs at affordable rates. As one of South India’s leading gold dealers, we're expanding nationwide and inviting you to grow with us. Become our franchisee and enjoy 38%–45% annual returns plus profit sharing—turning your dreams into a successful and profitable reality with SriStar Gold Company.

Mission and Vision

At SriStar Gold Company, our mission is to support individuals in achieving their dreams—whether it’s buying property, starting a business, or fulfilling personal goals—by offering instant cash against gold and precious metals at current market rates, including new, old, and scrap gold. Our vision extends beyond business growth; we strive to make India the global gold leader by promoting gold recycling, discouraging gold loans, and ensuring financial security for all. We aim to establish 100 franchises across smaller towns and cities, helping boost India’s economy through local entrepreneurship. By offering maximum gold value, rent-based gold jewellery for every occasion, and exclusively Made in India products, we promote pride, affordability, and self-reliance. We are committed to creating job opportunities across various roles like field executives, managers, designers, and more—contributing to employment and economic growth nationwide.

History and Milestones

SriStar Gold Company has launched 15+ branches across South India within just six months, thanks to your support. We offer the highest cash for gold—up to ₹10,000 more than online rates—valuing your trust and assets. With 200+ jobs created nationwide and a 60% women workforce, we proudly promote diversity, growth, and opportunity.
