Steps to Sell Gold

Follow These Easy Steps

  1. 1. Visit the nearest branch
    Walk into any of our Sristar branches. Our trained staff will welcome you and explain the gold selling process.
  2. 2. Test purity in front of you
    We use certified German machines to test your gold’s purity without any damage and right in front of you.
  3. 3. Get instant quote
    Based on current market rates and purity, we provide you with a best-price quote instantly.
  4. 4. Complete documentation
    Provide your valid ID proof like Aadhar or PAN. Our process is 100% legal and transparent.
  5. 5. Receive immediate payment
    Once everything is verified, you’ll receive payment instantly via cash, bank transfer, or UPI.
